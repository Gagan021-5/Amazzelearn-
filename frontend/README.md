# Amazze Learn PBL

Amazze Learn PBL is an interactive learning app designed to make school subjects feel active, visual, and hands-on instead of static and text-heavy.

Students explore concepts through guided simulations in science, mathematics, and social science. They can mix chemicals and study real reaction outcomes, build working circuits, test mathematical relationships, arrange historical events, and solve concept-based challenges with instant feedback.

The experience is built to feel clear, modern, and engaging across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices, so learners can experiment, observe, retry, and understand why an answer works.

At its core, this app is about turning learning into exploration:

- Science becomes a lab.
- Mathematics becomes something you can build and test.
- Social science becomes a story you can organize and understand.

It is made for students to learn by doing.
